Rummy Game Rules

Beta Version:

The game is currently in the Beta phase as we work diligently to address and resolve any bugs for a seamless gaming experience.

Gameplay Overview:

As you engage in Single-Player Rummy, a skill-based game, you'll experience strategic play, real-time updates, and daily tournaments with crypto prizes. The currency used in the tournaments is virtual.

Objective:

Your goal is to build a Meld with the 10 cards you are dealt. Draw a card from the deck and discard one from your hand until you have 10 Meld cards. The eleventh card is then discarded to end the game.

Declare Winner:

Click the "Declare Winner" button after achieving a winning hand. Discard the 11th card to finalize your victory. If an error occurs and there's no Winner Hand, the game is lost, and you can restart.

Leaderboard Scoring:

Track the number of cards in the discard pile. The Leaderboard considers the lowest number of cards in the discard pile as the winning criterion. After each tournament, the player with the fewest discarded cards emerges victorious.

Discard Pile Display:

At any time, the discard pile shows the number of discarded cards, providing transparency and insight into the competition.

Accepted Melds:

Melds can be either runs or sets. A run, such as A-2-3 or 8-9-10, must be in the same suit. Sets, like J-J-J-J, can have a minimum of 3 cards and a maximum of 4 cards.

Card Ranks Clarification:

Card ranks include Ace, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, and King. Note that runs like Ace-King-Queen are not valid, and Ace is exclusively used as 1.

Tournament Participation:

Players can engage in multiple games during a daily tournament, enhancing their chances of securing a position in the Prize ranks.

Anti-Abuse Measures:

Delays are implemented to prevent frequent game restarts. The restart button becomes clickable approximately 90 seconds after a game starts, and the "Declare Winner" button appears after a similar delay. Please refrain from refreshing the browser to maintain fair play.

Bug Reporting and Disputes:

If you encounter any bugs, feel free to report them. In the event of disputes, decisions made by us will be final and binding.

Enjoy the Game and Good Luck!
